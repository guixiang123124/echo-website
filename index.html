Echo 是免费的吗？ Is Echo free to use?

Apple 听写只给你原始转录。Echo 内置 470K+ 词智能键盘，四层 AI 润色可自动清理语法、去除口头禅、重写文本。而且 Echo 会随使用越来越准——你的专有名词和表达习惯都会被学习。 Apple Dictation gives you raw transcription. Echo includes a built-in smart keyboard for corrections, 4-layer AI polish to clean up grammar and filler words, and adaptive learning that gets more accurate over time.

支持中英文混合输入吗？ Does Echo support Chinese-English mix?

支持！Echo 擅长中英文代码切换，自动保留混合文本中的英文单词，支持 100+ 语言的语音转文字。 Yes! Echo excels at Chinese-English code-switching, automatically preserving English words in mixed-language text. It supports 100+ languages for transcription.
